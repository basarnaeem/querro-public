# Contributing & Contact

This is a public documentation repository for [Querro](https://querro.app), a structured intelligence platform for professional services deals. The product itself is closed source.

## What this repository accepts

This repository is a one-way publication of public-facing documentation. Pull requests, issues, and code contributions are not accepted at this time. This will change once the platform reaches general availability and a public API is published.

If you have spotted a factual error, a broken link, or a typo in any document here, you are welcome to email a correction directly. Please do not open issues on the public tracker for this purpose.

## Engagement principles

If you are reaching out about a customer pilot or partnership, three things help any conversation move quickly:

**1. Be specific about the deal type.** Querro is initially calibrated for US middle-market investment banking engagements — sell-side and buy-side M&A, debt capital raises, restructuring, ECM, private equity fund formation, and standalone advisory. If your firm operates outside this space, the platform will still be useful, but the question library tailored to your domain may not be in production yet.

**2. Be specific about volume.** The platform is most valuable for firms running multiple concurrent engagements. A boutique closing four to twelve transactions a year per banker is a strong fit. A firm running one large deal every eighteen months has a different cost-benefit calculus.

**3. Be specific about pain.** The most useful pilot conversations begin with a description of the current kickoff workflow — what works, what breaks, where the analyst hours are lost. Generic "I want to see a demo" inquiries are answered, but slowly.
